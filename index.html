About Us

Originally created by web developers at Moringa School hoping to become one of the most popular Tenant Website Application in East Africa.

The Tenant App gears towards ensuring that tenants have an easier way of meeting their neighbours and harmonising community living among the tenants.

This app also helps in ensuring security in the rentals in the sense that when i have an idea of who my neighbours are i will be able to identify them incase of an alert or any suspicious people around the rentals.

We hope it will bring a sense of community living among the tenants and at the end of the day increase security in the rentals.
